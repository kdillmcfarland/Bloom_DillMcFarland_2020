Help us buy a home

Honesty time. We are 30-somethings that have been living independently for a decade. We have a lot of stuff. What we are working toward is a house, a place where we can settle in and put holes wherever we want. Perhaps even paint a wall or have an outside plant! Thus, we humbly ask that if you choose to honor our wedding with a gift, you do so by helping use to purchase our first home. We are so excited to finally be moving toward this goal after many, many years of schooling and career unknowns!
